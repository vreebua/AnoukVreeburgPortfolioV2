Tools of choice

Adobe

The Adobe Suite is a place that I am vastly familiar with. For the last five years you can find me weekly in one of the programs. I love to make digital art in Illustrator, beautiful pictures in Photoshop, breathtaking animations in After Effects, smooth clips in Premiere Pro and a lot more. All these skills make for a very pretty end product!

Unity

I was first introduced to Unity through my minor Game Design, and I haven't stopped since. I always liked to code and to experiment, but Unity really brought it to a next level. I love how the program operates and the results I can get from it. It is very playful and allows me to farther develop my skills in C#.

Coding

After one coding class at my study I had already fallen in love. I love coding and the puzzles it presents that I got to solve. During my study I continuously chose directions and subjects that entailed coding. I quickly saw myself become better more and more that I have developed a vast amount of skills with it. From javascript to jQuery to C#. I just love the platform!
